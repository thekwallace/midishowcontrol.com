Privacy Policy

midishowcontrol.com is designed with your privacy in mind.

Overview

midishowcontrol.com is a browser-based tool for generating and sending MIDI Show Control cues. It operates entirely within your browser and does not collect or transmit personal data to external servers, with the exceptions noted below.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how the site is used. Analytics is only loaded if you accept cookies via the consent banner. If you decline, no analytics scripts are loaded and no tracking data is collected.

When accepted, Google Analytics may collect:

  • Page views and general usage patterns
  • Browser type and screen size
  • Approximate geographic region

No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics. You can change your preference by clearing your browser's local storage for this site.

Local Storage

Your settings and preferences (selected MIDI output device, device ID, command format, delay preferences, and analytics consent) are stored in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device.

MIDI Data

All MIDI Show Control data is generated and processed entirely within your browser:

  • Cue data is generated from your input and sent to your selected MIDI output device via Web MIDI
  • Exported MIDI files are created locally and downloaded directly to your computer
  • No MIDI data is transmitted to any server

Support Communications

If you contact us through the support form, your email address and message are submitted via Formspree and are used solely to respond to your inquiry. Formspree's own privacy policy applies to those submissions.

No Accounts Required

midishowcontrol.com does not require user accounts, passwords, or any form of registration. There is no user data stored on our servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. For questions, contact support@kwallace.com.
